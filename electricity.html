Electricity products

Smart house

Electricity comparison leads to savings? Our online comparison tool is the quickest and easiest way to review your options and find a cheaper energy deal. We’ll show you the discounts available, your estimated quarterly bill, and any other information you need to make the best choice for your household. Why else should you compare your electricity? To take advantage of a better plan – Your current energy plan may include discounts or lower rates but may revert to a more expensive type of plan when the contract ends. To see if there’s a better price available – Your bill is made up of both a supply charge and usage charges – both of which vary from retailer to retailer. This gives you the flexibility to shop around for cheaper rates. To review a range of plan discounts – A discount can be the path to a cheaper energy bill, but a short-term windfall may result in long-term pain if you don’t stick to the conditions of your contract (e.g. pay on time). To benefit from a change, we recommend you compare often; perhaps once a year.
